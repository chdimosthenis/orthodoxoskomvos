The **Church of Bulgaria** is one of the [autocephalous](/Autocephaly "Autocephaly") churches of the Orthodox Christian communion whose territory consists of the Republic of Bulgaria. The church is led by the Patriarch of All Bulgaria.

The primate of the Church of Bulgaria is His Holiness [Neofit (Dimitrov)](/Neofit_(Dimitrov)_of_Bulgaria "Neofit (Dimitrov) of Bulgaria"), Metropolitan of Sofia, Patriarch of All Bulgaria, who [enthroned](/Enthronement "Enthronement") on [February 24](/February_24 "February 24"), 2013, succeeding His Holiness [Maxim](/Maxim_(Minkov)_of_Bulgaria "Maxim (Minkov) of Bulgaria") who had reposed on [November 6](/November_6 "November 6"), 2012.

* Diocese of Sliven (Сливенска епархия)
* Diocese of Stara Zagora (Старозагорска епархия)
* Diocese of Plovdiv (Пловдивска епархия) [[4]](http://www.plovdivskamitropolia.bg)
